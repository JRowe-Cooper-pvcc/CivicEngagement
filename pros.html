Civic Engagement Project

Pros

Government regulation of the internet can serve various important functions, particularly when it comes to national security and public safety. Protecting National Security: Censorship can prevent access to sensitive information that could be exploited by foreign adversaries.

Limiting the Spread of Harmful Content: Governments can restrict access to extremist propaganda, cybercriminal networks, and illegal activities such as child exploitation.

Preventing Misinformation and Social Unrest: By controlling the spread of false information, governments can help maintain stability and prevent panic caused by misleading narratives.

While these measures can provide security and order, they come with concerns about overreach and abuse.
